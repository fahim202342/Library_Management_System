# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and maintainers of the **Library Management System** project, are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, respectful, and collaborative environment.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Treating everyone with respect and professionalism.
- Being kind, patient, and welcoming to new contributors.
- Respecting different opinions and constructive feedback.
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes and learning from them.
- Working together to improve the project and help the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or insulting comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any form of intimidation or abusive behavior.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional environment.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may take appropriate action against any behavior that violates these guidelines.
